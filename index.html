Amazing Features

DreamCapture uses your phone's built in speech-to-text technology and the natural language processing of IBM's Watson to make journaling your dreams a breeze.

Keep Records of Your Dreams

DreamCapture allows you to keep all your dream journal entries in one convienent place. Browse or search through your records, add notes, or tag them with keywords. It's never been so easy to keep your dreams organized.

Let Watson Do the Work

We use IBM's Watson API to perform natural language analysis on your journal entries. You'll get feedback on the overall sentiment of your entries, as well as recommended keywords to tag your dream with. Watson adds an extra layer of insight into your dreams!

Schedule Reminders to Record

Never forget your dreams again! With DreamCapture, you can set reminders to record your dream right when you wake up. Our notifications are silent, too - no need to worry about getting woken up too eary!
